Avalanche forecaster forecastes avalanche strikes using a combination of factors and artificial intelligence and save lives and damage loss caused by this strikes.As a user of our application you can use our application to access your location and following inputs from our data collectors,we enable you with the power to foresee the chances of an avalanche occurring. About
Disaster management is a critical use case and as responsible innovators we aim to use a number of calculations and techniques which includes a number of geographical information collected from our highly reliable and accurate sensors and satellites.After collecting these information in a secure encrypted way,it is given as input to our state of the art artificial Intelligence model which not only forecasts these parameters for the future but also classifies whether a avalanche might or might not happen for that day with a probability value.
